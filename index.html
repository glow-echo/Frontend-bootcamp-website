Frequently Asked Questions

Signing up for our courses is easy! Simply visit our website and navigate to the enrollment page. From there, you can select the course or program you're interested in and follow the prompts to complete the registration process. If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact our friendly customer support team for guidance. We're here to help you every step of the way!

Our courses are designed to accommodate students of all skill levels, from beginners to experienced developers. However, having a basic understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript can be beneficial for getting the most out of the learning experience. If you're new to frontend development, don't worry – our instructors will guide you through the fundamentals and help you build a strong foundation. Regardless of your current level of expertise, our courses are structured to challenge and empower you to succeed in mastering frontend development.

While we do not directly provide job placement services, we offer resources and support to help you in your job search. Our courses are designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the field of frontend development. Additionally, we offer guidance on resume building, portfolio creation, and interview preparation to help you stand out to potential employers. Our goal is to empower you with the tools and confidence to pursue exciting career opportunities in the tech industry.

We are located at Austrailia. Additionally, we offer virtual classes that you can attend from anywhere with an internet connection. Whether you prefer in-person or online learning, we have options to suit your needs. If you have any questions about our location or class formats, feel free to reach out to us for more information.

The cost of attending our frontend bootcamp varies depending on the specific course or program you choose, as well as any available discounts or promotions. We offer competitive pricing to ensure our courses are accessible to students from diverse backgrounds. For detailed pricing information and any current offers, please visit our website or contact our customer support team. We're here to assist you in finding the best option that fits your budget and learning goals.
